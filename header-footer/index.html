Solutions

Despite significant increases in appropriations and a decline in Veteran populations, fulfilling our nation’s promise to our heroes is problematic. To this end, we are modernizing VA through innovation to solve the hardest problems impacting VA and our Veterans.

Transportation: A key social determinant of health.

Transportation challenges affect rural and urban communities.

Each year, 3.6 million people in the United States do not obtain medical care due to transportation issues. Transportation issues include lack of vehicle access, inadequate infrastructure, long distances and lengthy times to reach needed services, transportation costs and adverse policies that affect travel.

Explore Veterans transportation benefits

Reference: Social Determinants of Health Series (AHA/HRET Guides)

VA Innovation Center (VIC) initiatives are designed to ensure the success of
innovation initiatives authorized by VA MISSION Act, Section 152. 1703E.
Portfolio activities are interdependent, integrate efforts and maximize impact.

Care and Payment Innovation

Mission Act Section 152 1703E

Developing innovative approaches to testing payment and service delivery models in order to reduce expenditures while preserving or enhancing the quality of care furnished by the department.

Joint Ventures and Initiatives

Mutually Valuable Agreements

Maximizing VA assets by leveraging resources of our public and industry partners while focusing on mission-critical issues. We prioritize big, bold ideas with the potential to deliver permanent solutions to persistent, systemic, VA and Veteran issues.

Learn more »

Open Innovation Catalyst

Wisdom of the Crowd

Innovating with external ideas and competition oriented, dynamic, financing mechanisms. Collective and creative problem solving by convening subject matter experts and multi-disciplinary teams with the common goal of solving curated VA and Veteran problems.

Learn more »

Internal Innovation Consulting

Ideation with VA Leaders

Facilitating root cause analysis engagements with leaders across the enterprise to evaluate and prioritize issues that could be resolved by sustaining or disruptive innovation.

Learn more »

Innovation Brain Trust

Leading Edge Insights

Enabling high-value collaboration with technical and topical subject matter experts from across the globe. Technology focused subcommittees of the VIC Innovation Advisory Taskforce will be led by Innovators-in-Residence.
