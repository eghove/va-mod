/ VA TELEHEALTH
VA Telehealth changes the location of routine VA health care services and
allows Veterans to receive care for 50+ clinical specialties, ensuring the right
care, in the right place, at the right time. It leverages health informatics,
disease management, and technology improving access to care.
+1,000,000 19%
from 2017
Telehealth Visits in 2018
VA's telehealth capabilities are bridging the care gap
through technology that gives Veterans
access to the timely, quality care they deserve,
without traveling great distances to a VA facility.
Time spent traveling is time away from Veterans'
jobs and families.

- VA Secretary Robert Wilkie



VA Mission

To fulfill President Lincoln’s promise “To care for him who shall have borne the battle, and for his widow, and his orphan” by serving and honoring the men and women who are America’s Veterans.

We’re here anytime, day or night - 24/7

If you are a Veteran in crisis or concerned about one, connect with our caring, qualified responders for confidential help. Many of them are Veterans themselves.

  • Call 1-800-273-8255 and press 1
  • Text to 838255
  • Chat confidentially now
  • Call TTY if you have hearing loss 1-800-799-4889

Get more resources at VeteransCrisisLine.net.
