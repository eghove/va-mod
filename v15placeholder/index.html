/ VA TELEHEALTH

VA Telehealth changes the location of routine VA health care services and allows Veterans to receive care for 50+ clinical specialties, ensuring the right care, in the right place, at the right time. It leverages health informatics, disease management, and technology improving access to care.

VA's telehealth capabilities are bridging the care gap through technology that gives Veterans access to the timely, quality care they deserve, without traveling great distances to a VA facility. Time spent traveling is time away from Veterans' jobs and families.

- ROBERT WILKIE, VA SECRETARY

/ ABOUT US
Our Mission is Delivering a Stronger Future by transforming the VA into a modern, high-performing organization that simplifies operations and achieves high-quality service and care for Veterans.
About VA Modernization

VA Modernization is a continuous journey that empowers our Veterans, employees, and partners to:

  • Transform systems to create foundational change
  • Simplify operations to improve customer service
  • Achieve better results for Veterans
VA Modernization Leadership

Chief Modernization Officer Surafeal Asgedom leads efforts to transform VA through private/public partnerships, emerging technologies, and business integration. The Modernization Office leverages best-in-class processes and tools to enable enterprise-level modernization.

Modernization Priorities
  • Customer Service
    Exceptional Veterans experience
  • MISSION ACT
    The best care from VA and the community providers
  • Business Transformation
    System modernization and employee empowerment
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) Modernization
    Seamless transition of care after military service
Managing Business Change

As the champions of transformation, the Modernization Office is managing change, so people embrace, adopt, and use the solutions. Integrating business and technical solutions requires transforming multiple enterprise activities simultaneously.

Achieving Results

Modernization delivers a stronger future.

  • Veteran Trust in VA increased from 47% to 87%
  • VA Healthcare wait times shorter than private sector
  • The new VA.GOV simplifies the experience for Veterans
  • VA’s Appeals Modernization Act decreasing wait times and improving Veteran satisfaction

Digital Transformation

Delivers superior digital customer service by simplifying the Veteran experience through digital platforms that achieve the highest levels of Customer Service, Effectiveness, and Ease of Use

Electronic Health Record Modernization

Enables Veterans to receive high-reliability healthcare through the deployment of an Enterprise-wide Integrated Health Information System by transforming 140 de-centralized, non-connected programs into one network, so providers make better decisions that lead to improved outcomes

Appeals Modernization

Allows Veterans to receive Accurate and Timely Appeal Resolution through the Implementation of the Appeals Modernization Act giving Veterans greater choice and control when they disagree with VA’s decision, with three review options: Higher Level Review, Supplemental Claim, or a Board Appeal

Supply Chain Modernization

Delivers the Right Items, To the Right Place, At the Right Time, In the Right Condition by transforming the enterprise VA supply chain through a robust supply-chain training and development program, integrated data analysis, and comprehensive equipment lifecycle management program

Financial Management Business Transformation

Improves financial stewardship by transforming the financial and acquisition business process and systems to achieve standardized financial and acquisition practices, cost savings, stronger internal controls, and the reduction of risk

Delivering Integrated Care

VA consistently delivers high-quality health care and customer service. We will restore trust by consistently delivering reliable, quality care; create a learning organization where employees continually improve; and modernize systems by using the tools required to deliver the best care.

Integrated High Reliability Organization (HRO)

VA will lead the way to transform the nation's largest integrated health care system into the largest high-functioning HRO in the world. VA is implementing procedures and protocols that maximize safety and minimize harm, ensuring every patient receives excellent care, every time.

Excellence in American Healthcare

We deliver higher-quality and safer health care than the private sector. A study published in the Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA) concluded VA primary care wait times are shorter than those in the private sector in 15 major metropolitan areas.

Increasing Veterans' Access to Health Care

The MISSION Act strengthens VA's ability to deliver trusted, easy to access, high quality care at VA facilities, virtually through telehealth, and in your community. That means you get the care and services you need, where and when you need them.

/ VA TECHNOLOGY
Pursuing high-tech healthcare.
The VA's commitment to cutting-edge technologies takes many forms
and Veterans are already seeing the benefits.
VA 3D Printing
The emerging innovations of 3D printing are being used in areas as varied as pre-surgical planning, the manufacture of hand and foot orthotics, prosthesis prototyping, and more
VA Interoperability
VA is creating and implementing a single longitudinal clinical health record, from active duty to Veteran status, to ensure interoperability.
VA APIs
VA recently launched the Health Application Programming Interface (API) to accelerate the creation of digital tools to support Veterans. Veterans will soon have access to their personal medical data using the Health Records iPhone feature.
VA Artificial Intelligence
VA announced a medical research partnership to analyze patterns from 700,000 historical health records to develop machine learning algorithms that will accurately identify risk factors for patient deterioration and predict its onset.
